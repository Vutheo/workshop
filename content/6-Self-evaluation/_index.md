### General Evaluation

**1. Learning Environment**  
The learning and working environment is generally friendly and open. Members of the First Cloud Journey program are approachable and always willing to support when I face difficulties during the learning process. The learning space is also quite comfortable, which helps me stay focused while completing assigned tasks. If possible, the program could include more networking or group sharing activities to improve interaction among participants.

**2. Mentor / Admin Support**  
The mentor provides clear and detailed guidance, is always willing to clarify anything I do not understand, and encourages me to try solving problems on my own before giving direct instructions. The admin team also provides quick support regarding documents, information, and program-related issues, ensuring the learning process runs smoothly without interruptions.

**3. Relevance to Academic Background**  
The content of the program is quite aligned with what I have studied at university, especially in areas related to cloud computing and system fundamentals. At the same time, the program also introduces many practical topics that I had not previously encountered, helping me better understand how AWS services operate in real-world environments.

**4. Learning and Skill Development Opportunities**  
During the program, I have gained several important skills such as working in a structured process, researching documentation, teamwork, and communication in a professional learning environment. In addition, exposure to real-world exercises and scenarios has helped me improve my critical thinking and problem-solving abilities.

**5. Learning Culture & Team Spirit**  
I find the learning environment in the program to be quite positive. Everyone respects and supports each other throughout the learning process. Even when deadlines are tight, the collaborative spirit remains strong, which helps me feel more comfortable and motivated to participate.

**6. Learning Support Activities in the Program**  
In the First Cloud Journey program, I was able to participate in internal guidance and training sessions, which were very helpful in helping me better understand how AWS services are applied in practice. In addition, the provided materials and clear learning roadmap made it easier for me to follow and complete assigned tasks. Overall, the learning support activities are quite complete and suitable for beginners.
